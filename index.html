about me
Hi. I’m Nick, and I’m thrilled you’re here. I like technology and the way it shapes culture, enhances quality of life, and propels us to a better society. However, technology for the sake of itself is unsustainable. We need strong ethics to fulfill the promises we make about our products while staying true to the human issues that inspired us. This is my goal: to advance society by developing technology that speaks as well as listens; that progresses our capabilities while respecting our values; that works toward a better future for us all. I’m currently a product manager at TapToBook and recently graduated from Brown University, where I got my B.S. in computer science. I focused on artificial intelligence and human-computer interaction while keeping the humanities close to heart. I've lived across the United States and the U.K. I love to travel and explore new cultures. Find me daydreaming about my next adventure.
